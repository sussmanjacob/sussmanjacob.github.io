About Me

Hi, my name is Jacob! I am a first-year Biostatistics PhD student at the Duke University School of Medicine. I'm broadly interested in applying quantitative and computational methods to questions in epidemiology and public health. I have interests in clinical machine learning, spatial statistics, and high-dimensional statistics. I'm most driven by applications that consider public health in a social context, and focus on health justice.

Research Interests

  • Clinical Machine Learning
  • Spatial Statistics
  • Algorithmic Fairness/Bias
  • Infectious Disease Epidemiology
